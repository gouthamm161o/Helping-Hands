The Helping Hands Foundation started as a CAS Project by IB Students from Ecole Mondiale World School. The Helping Hands Foundation is a voluntary organization and is always in need of your help. While we have our trustees and sponsors, we still need volunteers to help us execute our plans.The Helping Hands Foundation has facilitated more than 100,000 masks, 15000 plus PPE kits and over 200,000 units of food items and are providing shelter to over 200 families at 3 shelter homes. There is more work in progress.As part of it, the Volunteers of HHH would be taking tuitions to help the children complete their homework and they also look after the basic needs of the children and do continuous follow ups regarding health and education of the child.

Our mission is to provide accommodation to every single child who comes to Mumbai for cancer treatment thereby ensuring zero abandonment due to lack of accommodation. The Accommodation Project provides free accommodation to the child and the family during the child's cancer treatment.

After working with economically weaker sections in medical distress for over a decade - Helping Hand Foundation, one of the leading non-government public health organization has consciously embarked on a more sustainable model of linking the common man to public health system while continuing to support patients in medical exigencies.Our mission is to provide accommodation to every single child who comes to Mumbai for cancer treatment thereby ensuring zero abandonment due to lack of accommodation.
